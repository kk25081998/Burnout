Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 8/16/2023

At Flourish at Work, we value your privacy. This policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your personal information. By using our platform, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal and non-personal information when you use our platform. This includes:

  • Personal Information: This is information that can be used to identify you. Examples include your name, email address, and workplace details.
  • Non-Personal Information: This refers to data that cannot be used to identify you personally, such as browser types, device types, and time-stamps.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, conduct research, and communicate with you.

3. Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except to provide services you have requested, with your permission, or as required by law.

4. Cookies and Trackers

Our platform may use "cookies" to enhance user experience. You can choose to set your browser to refuse cookies, but doing so might affect certain functionality of the platform.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the protection of your personal information. While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we implement robust measures to protect against unauthorized access.

6. Access and Control of Personal Data

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Please contact us to make such requests.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about our practices.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
