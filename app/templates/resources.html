Try a Relaxing Activity

Relaxing activities are not just a way to pass time; they're essential for mental health. Activities like meditation, yoga, or tai chi can help reduce stress, improve focus, and enhance overall well-being. By incorporating these practices into your routine, you can create a balance between work and relaxation.
